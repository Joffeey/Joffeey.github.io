Welcome to VEPS - Vertical Environmental Parking Solutions


Our project's problem was presented to us by Bikeloop. Bikeloop's goal is to enhance the user experience and safety within bicycle parking.


Their wish is to develop a smart bicycle stand that ensures efficient and safe storage, while also enhancing the user experience. The goal is to create an innovative and space-efficient parking solution equipped with user-friendly locking & unlocking features as well as charging possibilities.


The parking solution should be able to contain up to 10 bicycles, safely storing them vertically with a locking mechanism that opens, closes, lifts, and lowers the bicycle. The design should be flexible enough to be installed both under a roof and outside, e.g., on sidewalks.


Further security measures include possibilities for further securing of the bicycle, storage room for battery, and other items. The final solution shall be integrated with Bikeloop's current application and platform, delivering a whole, modern parking experience for the users.


The parking solution should:

  • Be able to contain up to 10 bicycles, safely storing them vertically.
  • Include a locking mechanism that opens, closes, lifts, and lowers the bicycle.
  • Be flexible enough for installation under a roof or outside, e.g., on sidewalks.

Further security measures include:

  • Possibilities for further securing of the bicycle.
  • Storage room for batteries and other items.
